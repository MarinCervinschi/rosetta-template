---
title: Explanation
description: Understanding-oriented discussion that illuminates context, rationale, and trade-offs.
---

Explanation is **the why**. It is reflective writing about design choices, alternatives considered, and the shape of the problem space. It does not solve problems and does not teach skills — it builds a mental model.

Use explanation when the reader keeps asking "but *why* is it like this?". Good explanation makes reference pages feel obvious and tutorials feel grounded.
