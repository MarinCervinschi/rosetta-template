---
title: How-to guides
description: Problem-oriented recipes that answer a specific question for someone who already knows the basics.
category: how-to
---

How-to guides are **recipes**. They assume a competent reader with a real, named problem and walk them to a solution as directly as possible. They skip background theory and do not attempt to teach.

Write a how-to when the reader already knows *what* they want and just needs to know *how*. Title it with the goal ("How to deploy to production", not "Deployment").
