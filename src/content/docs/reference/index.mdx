Reference material is **the map**. It is dry, structured, and exhaustive. It describes *what exists*, not how to use it and not why. Readers arrive with a specific lookup in mind and leave with an answer.

Prefer tables, field-by-field listings, and precise types. Avoid narrative, opinion, and examples beyond the minimum needed to clarify shape.
