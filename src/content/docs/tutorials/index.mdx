---
title: Tutorials
description: Learning-oriented guides that take a newcomer from zero to their first working result.
---

Tutorials are **lessons**. They hold the reader's hand through a complete, end-to-end experience and leave them with a sense of accomplishment. They are *not* references, and they do *not* solve arbitrary problems — they teach skills in a safe, curated environment.

Start a tutorial when the reader needs to *learn by doing*. Keep it linear, name every step, and promise a concrete result at the end.
